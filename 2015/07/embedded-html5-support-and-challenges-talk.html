HTML5 Developer Support and Challenges Conference Talk

Jul 5, 2015 • Comments

Tweet

On 3rd July 2015, I gave a conference talk, along with Andrea Fassina at the ARM’s Embedded HTML5 Workshop at DTG in Vauxhall, London.

In the presentation, Andrea explained our approach to securely enabling app development on production set top boxes at YouView and an investigation into the performance of various Javascript libraries and frameworks on YouView devices. I then talked through the findings of our investigation into speeding up the launch times of HTML5 apps on the platform and gave some general best practices for app development.
